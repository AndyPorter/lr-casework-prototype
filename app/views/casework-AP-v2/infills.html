Estate Info: Absolute Freehold Estate in Land

Address: Rosebrae Court, Woodside Ferry Approach, Birkenhead, (CH41 6DU)

A: Property register

This register describes the land and estate comprised in the title.

  1. (29.11.2002) The Freehold land shown edged with red on the plan of the above Title filed at the Registry and being Rosebrae Court, Woodside Ferry Approach, Birkenhead, (CH41 6DU).

  2. (29.11.2002) The Conveyance dated 4 October 1960 referred to in the Charges Register contains the following exceptions and reservations:-

    "EXCEPTING AND RESERVING unto the persons entitled thereto:-

    (i) any mines or minerals under the property hereby conveyed or any right of support from any mines or minerals thereunder whatsoever

    Unto the Commission:-

    (ii) Any easement or right of light air or support or other easement or right which would restrict or interfere with the free use by the Commission or any person deriving title under them for building or any other purpose of any adjoining or neighbouring land of the Commission (whether intended to be retained or to be sold by them)

    (iii) The right at any time to erect or suffer to be erected any buildings or other erections and to alter any building or other erection now standing or hereafter to be erected on any part of the adjoining or neighbouring land of the Commission in such manner as to obstruct or interfere with the passage of light or air to any building which is or may be erected upon the property hereby conveyed and any access of light or air over the adjoining land of the Commission shall be deemed to be enjoyed by the licence or consent of the Commission as of right

    (iv) The right of support from the property hereby conveyed to the adjoining property of the Commission

    (v) The right to have maintain use reconstruct alter and remove any pipes wires cables and works on over or under the property hereby conveyed now used for the benefit of the adjoining property of the Commission

    (vi) The free right of passage and running of water and soil from any part of the adjoining land of the Commission through any sewers drains and watercourses which may hereafter be constructed under the property with full right and liberty in perpetuity but in common with the Purchasers and their successors in title (and all other persons having a like right) to make connections with and drain into such sewers drains or watercourses in connection with and for the purposes of the drainage of the railway and works of the Commission

    (vii) The full right and liberty to have and maintain on their adjoining land a signal box and main station and auxiliary buildings in the positions at present occupied thereby part or parts whereof overhang the property and all rights of eavesdrop and other easements and quasi easements in respect thereof shall be maintained as they existed immediately prior to the execution hereof

    (viii) The full right and liberty for the Commission and their successors in title with or without workmen at all reasonable times to enter upon the property hereby conveyed for the purpose of exercising all or any of the rights reserved by paragraphs (v) and (vi) of this sub-clause and for the purposes of repairing and cleansing any sewers drains and watercourses and for the purpose of maintaining repairing renewing reinstating altering or amending the said signal box and main station and auxiliary buildings and any fences walls bridges and other works of the Commission on their adjoining or neighbouring land

    (ix) The right to enter upon the property hereby conveyed at any time to use the hydrants the property of the Commission situate on the south west side of some parts of the property hereby conveyed."

    NOTE: This registration takes effect subject thereto and the mines and minerals so excepted are excluded from the registration of the land hatched blue on the filed plan.

  3. (29.11.2002) The land has the benefit of the following rights reserved by but is subject to the following rights granted by a Transfer of adjoining land and other land dated 11 April 1997 made between (1) The Merseyside Development Corporation and (2) Highcrag Limited:-

    "TOGETHER WITH AND EXCEPTING AND RESERVING as mentioned in the Schedule hereto.

    SCHEDULE

    PART 2

    (RIGHTS GRANTED)

    The following rights are granted to the Purchaser for the benefit of the property and for the owner for the time being of the property in connection with the use of the property such rights being exercised in common with the Corporation and those authorised by the Corporation (so far as the Corporation has power to grant the same) the right to connect to and use the conduits serving the Property laid under the said roadway or pathways but not so as to overload the same and the right (until the same are adopted) to enter and break up the said roadway or pathways for the purposes of making such connections the Purchaser making good to the reasonable satisfaction of the Corporation all damage caused and causing as little disturbance and inconvenience as possible.

    PART 3

    (Exceptions and Reservations)

    EXCEPTING AND RESERVING unto the Corporation and all others from time to time authorised by the Corporation or entitled to the like right:-

    (1) the right to the free passage of water soil gas electricity and telephone services through the conduits which are now in upon or under the Property or which may be subsequently installed or constructed within the Perpetuity Period

    (2) the right to enter onto the Property for the purpose of constructing inspecting repairing maintaining renewing or replacing any of the sewers pipes wires cables or other service media as aforesaid the person exercising such right causing as little damage and inconvenience as possible and making good to the reasonable satisfaction of the Purchaser any damage to the Property thereby caused

    (3) the right to enter onto the Property in so far as may be reasonably necessary for the purpose of repairing maintaining or decorating any buildings or structures from time to time erected on the Corporation's Estate or for the purpose of executing any works of development of whatsoever nature thereon the person exercising such right causing as little inconvenience as possible and making good to the reasonable satisfaction of the Purchaser any damage to the Property thereby caused

    (4) the right to develop or permit the development of the Corporation's Estate or any part thereof in any way whatsoever without payment of compensation of any nature whatsoever notwithstanding that the access of light or air to the Property may thereby be impeded or restricted in any way

    (5) all rights of light air support shelter and protection for as shall now or hereafter belong to and be enjoyed by any land or building adjacent to the Property."

  4. (29.11.2002) The land has the benefit of the following rights reserved by the Transfer dated 11 March 1998 referred to in the Charges Register:-

    "excepting and reserving as provided for in Scheule 2

    SCHEDULE 2

    (Rights reserved)

    Excepting and reserving unto the Transferor and all others from time to time authorised by the Transferor or otherwise entitled to the like right

    (1) the full and free right to the uninterrupted passage and running of water soil gas electricity telephone and other services through the sewers pipes wires cables and other media laid or within the Perpetuity Period to be laid in on under or over the Property

    (2) the right to enter onto the Property for the purpose of constructing connecting into inspecting repairing maintaining renewing or replacing any of the sewers pipes wires cables or other service media as aforesaid the person exercising such right causing as little damage and inconvenience as possible and making good any damage to the Property thereby caused

    (3) the right to enter onto the Property in so far as may reasonably be necessary for the purpose of repairing maintaining or decorating any buildings or structures from time to time erected on the Transferor's Estate or for the purpose of executing any works of development of whatsoever nature thereon the person exercising such right causing as little inconvenience as possible and making good any damage to the Property thereby caused

    (4) the right to develop or permit the development of the Transferor's Estate or any part thereof in any way whatsoever without payment of compensation of any nature whatsoever notwithstanding that the access of light or air to the Property may thereby be impeded or restricted in any way.

  5. (29.11.2002) The land has the benefit of the rights granted by but is subject to the rights reserved by the Transfer dated 6 January 1992 referred to in the Charges Register.

  6. (29.11.2002) The Transfer dated 6 January 1992 referred to in the Charges Register contains provisions as to light or air and boundary structures.
